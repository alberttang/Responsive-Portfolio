About Me

Albert Tangtheinkul is an experienced digital marketer, dog enthusiast, member of the NRA, and can drive anything with wheels. He is currently the digital marketing director at Pixel Motion, a digital marketing agency specializing in automotive retailing, and is developing his web development skills at the coding bootcamp at UCI.

While not working, he enjoys hanging out with his dogs Brindy(pictured) and Kiki, both rescue dogs from the Baldwin Park Animal Shelter, or practicing his marksmanship at the local indoor shooting range. He also has a passion for cars and motorcycles and enjoys going to local meets and car shows. He currently resides in Walnut, CA and would love to connect with like minded individuals.
